01 — The Trainer

Meet Jane

Jane Coates Leckie has spent her life moving — and for years now, she's spent it helping other people move better, too. As an ACE-Certified Personal Trainer, Jane built BodyByJane around one simple belief: an active lifestyle is the foundation for confidence, longevity, and genuine happiness.

Training with Jane isn't about chasing a number on a scale or surviving a brutal workout. It's about building a body and a routine that lets you actually live the life you want — whether that's keeping up with your kids, feeling strong in your own skin, or just walking onto the beach without a second thought.

Sessions are personal by design. Jane works with clients one-on-one and in small groups, in the studio, on the sand, in your home gym, or out on a trail — wherever movement feels good and fits your life.

"My goal isn't to give you a workout — it's to help you build a life you feel strong enough to live."
